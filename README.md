    <h1>Privacy Policy for CinemaShelf</h1>
    <p><strong>Last updated:</strong> May 14, 2026</p>

    <h2>Overview</h2>
    <p>CinemaShelf ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal movie and series tracking application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.</p>

    <h2>Data Collection</h2>
    <p>CinemaShelf stores all your data (movies, series, notes, ratings) <strong>locally on your device only</strong>. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.</p>

    <h2>Advertising</h2>
    <p>The app uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use data as described in <a href="https://policies.google.com/privacy">Google's Privacy Policy</a>. This may include:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Device identifiers</li>
        <li>IP address</li>
        <li>Usage data for ad personalization</li>
    </ul>
    <p>You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Google → Ads.</p>

    <h2>Data Storage</h2>
    <p>All user-created content (movie lists, notes, ratings) is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.</p>

    <h2>Data Sharing</h2>
    <p>We do not share your personal data with any third parties. The "Share" feature in the app only shares your golden list text via your device's built-in sharing mechanism (email, messaging apps, etc.) at your explicit request.</p>

    <h2>Children's Privacy</h2>
    <p>CinemaShelf is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.</p>

    <h2>Changes to This Policy</h2>
    <p>We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.</p>

    <h2>Contact</h2>
    <p>If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: <strong>vpola0049@gmail.com</strong></p>
